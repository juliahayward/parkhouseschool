Aims

The Sleeping Dragons programme aims to:

  • Set developmentally appropriate goals on an individual basis for the children. These goals would be in the therapeutically relevant areas of socialisation, communication, behaviour, cognitive skills, play and self-help skills.
  • Ensure the goals are practical and realistic: i.e. that those from one meeting can be achieved before the subsequent meeting, typically in 2 months' time.
  • Help parents get a better understanding of what they need to achieve with their children, and of various methods which could help them to do this.
  • Unify the goals of the different people who may be working with each child. This ensures that contradictory advice is not given, and that parents are not overloaded with suggestions.
  • Provide a forum for monitoring and updating children’s progress on a regular basis.
  • Provide a multidisciplinary forum for problem solving, allowing for input from lots of people who knew the child.
